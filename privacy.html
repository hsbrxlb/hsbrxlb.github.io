The Quibit platform respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how Quibit collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use the Quibit mobile application and related services (the "Service").

Data Type Examples Purpose
Account Information Email, password, username Account creation and authentication
Profile Information Bio, interests, photos, MBTI personality type, school/work info (all optional) Personalization and user connections

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, time spent
  • Log Information: IP address, error logs, performance data

1.3 Information from Third Parties

Quibit may receive information from:

Quibit uses your information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core Service Functions

  • Activity Matching: Show you relevant activities within walking distance
  • User Connections: Help you find and connect with nearby users
  • Communication: Enable messaging between connected users
  • Safety: Maintain a safe community environment

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Develop new functionality based on user needs
  • Fix bugs and enhance performance
  • Conduct research and analytics

2.3 Business Operations

  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service updates
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Location Data and Privacy

Location is Core to Our Service: Quibit is fundamentally a location-based app. We use your precise location to show you nearby activities and users within a 0.5-mile radius.

3.1 How We Use Location

  • Display activities and users near your current location
  • Calculate distances for activity recommendations
  • Enable location-based search and filtering
  • Improve local content relevance

3.2 Location Controls

  • You can disable location services in your device settings
  • Disabling location will significantly limit app functionality
  • We don't store detailed location history
  • Location sharing with other users is always your choice

6. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

Your Rights Include:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities

6.1 Account Controls

  • Edit your profile and privacy settings in the app
  • Control who can see your activities and profile
  • Manage your notification preferences
  • Delete your account and associated data

You can delete your account at any time through the app settings. When you delete your account:
